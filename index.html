Insight 2k19

INSIGHT the technical fest hosted by the students of CCSIT of Calicut university and department of computer science is a celebration of intelligence and knowledge combined to forge a new world of technology. It provides a platform for the students to explore and enhance their technical skills and also bestows opportunity to endeavour beyond the monotonous academic curriculum. This jubilee puts forth a rostrum for 4000+ students from different colleges to conduct seminars, exhibitions, workshops, technical and cultural events. To this gathering we extend our warm welcome to participate and enjoy the events.
Every year INSIGHT is concentrating on a relevant area of the Information Technology. Therefore the programme will be conducted based on a particular theme. The focus area of this edition of INSIGHT is INTERNET ENGINEERING.

    Technova v4.0

    The major attraction of INSIGHT is TECHNOVA. The aim of this event is to find out the best geek. It consists of five rounds. Topper of each round will be awarded with cash prizes. Winner of the the last round will get the grand prize.

    The competition is conducted via an online web application. The questions will be of different types including multiple choice and descriptive.

    Rules
    • Each group can contain a minimum of one and a maximum of five members.
    • Participants must bring laptops.
    • Participants are allowed to use the Internet and other resources; free Wi-Fi at the venue is expected, but not guaranteed.
    • There might be multiple levels for each round.
    • There will not be a knock out stage until the 4th round.

    Rounds
    • One - Cleverness, logic and general knowledge
    • Two - Basic knowledge of computer and Internet usage
    • Three - Basic programming and debugging skills
    • Four - Intermediate programming, scripting and debugging skills
    • Five - Advanced programming and scripting skills and cryptanalysis

    Prize: 30000
    Reg fee: 200 ( per head )
    Venue: Seminar Complex, Nucleus
    2:00 pm, 11 april 2019

    Papilo Prezantim

    The intention of the event is to find out best Orator.

    Rules and Regulations
    • Two members or less per team.
    • The team members must be from the same college.
    • Participants can select any latest technical topic
    • Maximum 4 papers can send from one college/centre for paper presentation
    • Participants should send synopsis of their topic to cuinsight2k19@gmail.com on or before 3th April 2019
    • The accepted paper/abstract will be announced through official website on 8th April 2019

    Team
    • A group of maximum 2 members can participate.
    • The same person cannot be the part of more than one group.
    • The group members should be of same college.
    • Individuals can also participate.

    Guidelines for Paper/Abstract
    • Only accepted in PDF format.
    • Paper/Abstract must be in IEEE format.

    Guidelines for Final Presentation
    • Participant must bring one copy of the paper for judgment purpose and slides of 15 minutes + 5 minutes (Judges or Questioner).
    • Projector and Laptop will be provided.

    Prize: 6000
    Reg fee: 150
    Venue: Seminar Complex, Electron
    10:00am, 12 april 2019

    Mock It Up

    The intention of the event is to provide exact experience of a placement cell.

    Rules
    • This event is an individual event.
    • The usages of mobile phones are strictly prohibited.
    • There will be three rounds.
    • This event is exclusively for CCSIT Centres.

    Round 1 (Aptitude Test):
    • There will be 50 multiple choice questions to be completed within the time span of 45 minutes.
    • The candidates will be selected based on cut-off mark.

    Round 2 (Group discussion):
    • This round will be based on general current affairs.
    • The duration of this round is 30 – 45 minutes.
    • 5 – 8 candidates will be selected for the next round.

    Round 3 (Personal Interview):
    • Candidates who cleared GD are selected for this round.
    • One candidate will be selected as winner.

    Reg fee: 30 ( per head )
    Venue: Seminar Complex, Electron
